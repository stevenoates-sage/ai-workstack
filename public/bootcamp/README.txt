QUICK START
───────────
  1. Open AI_Analyst_Weekend_Bootcamp.html in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  2. Complete the Pre-Work section (20–30 minutes).
     This installs VS Code, the Claude Code extension, and sets up your
     project folder.
  3. Copy all three CSV files into your project folder:
       Documents\ai-analyst\
  4. Follow the Saturday session (Phases 1 & 2), then Sunday (Phases 3–5).

ABOUT THE DATA FILES
────────────────────
  All three CSV files contain realistic but entirely fictional GTM sales
  data. No real company information is included. They are designed to
  simulate a typical B2B SaaS sales organization with multiple regions,
  verticals, and reps.

  • gtm_pipeline.csv — Individual deal/opportunity records with stages,
    ARR values, dates, sources, and win/loss outcomes.
  • monthly_bookings.csv — Aggregated monthly bookings by region and
    vertical, including new logo, expansion, and renewal ARR.
  • rep_performance.csv — Quarterly performance metrics for 20 sales
    reps across 6 quarters, including quota attainment, win rates, and
    activity levels.

NOTES
─────
  • This is a self-paced course. There is no instructor or live session.
  • No coding experience is required. Claude Code writes all the code.
  • All work stays on your local machine — nothing is uploaded or shared.
  • The training document includes clickable links to all referenced
    resources, videos, and tools.
